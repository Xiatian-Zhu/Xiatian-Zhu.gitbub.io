    Xiatian Zhu

Xiatian Zhu is a Computer Vision Researcher at Vision Semantics Ltd., Queen Mary University of London, United Kindom. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Vision from the Queen Mary University of London, M.Eng. and B.Eng. in Computer Science from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. His research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition and machine (deep) learning.
