    Xiatian Zhu

Xiatian Zhu is Postdoctoral Research Assistant in the Queen Mary University of London, working with Dr. Tao Xiang and Prof. Shaogang Gong. He received his Ph.D. (2015) in Computer Vision from the Queen Mary University of London, M.Eng. and B.Eng. in Computer Science from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. He servers as reviewer for major computer vision and machine learning journals (International Journal of Computer Vision, Journal of Machine Learning Research, IEEE Trans. Image Processing, IEEE Trans. on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, Neural Networks, Image and Vision Computing, Computer Vision and Image Understanding, Neurocomputing, Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence) and conference (British Machine Vision Conference). His research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition and machine learning.
